Hello!

My name is Owen Forloine, and I am a 15 year old Web Designer. I have constructed many digital products with Visual Studio and FinalSite. I start my ideas by sketching them out on paper, then I structure my page to be convenient for the user. I am excited to make new projects in the near future and improve the ones I have already created. I plan on changing the world through the way we perceive software and web pages, by growing a team of outstanding programmers and people who constantly think of great ideas I can put to use. This, as well as involve myself in clubs and organizations to further boost my potential.

Growing up in Worthington Ohio, I have always wanted to learn how software works. There have been numerous poorly made websites that I felt a need to renovate. I decided to get a book on basic coding languages and set to work. I made small websites such as calculators and weather apps, but then I decided to create something big. My dad is a retirement planner and we would do stock compeitions together. But this was quite inconvenient since we used a Google Sheet. I wanted to create a way to use fake money to still invest into real stocks. That is how I decided to build a stock simulator. Now I am making sites left and right, and hopefully I can begin creating software rather than web pages soon!
